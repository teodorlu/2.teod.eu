Structural and direct?

Lispers like to work on structural expressions, so called s-expressions. Writers need a low-friction writing surface. Write, and you've got text on screen or paper. Nonlinear writers may follow in the shoes of Ted Nelson and declare themselves unsatisfied with the shackles of single-directed texts. Where is branching?
